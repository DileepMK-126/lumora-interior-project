## Individual Project Showcase

Use this structure for every project page.

Hero:

- Fullscreen project image
- Title, category, location, year, scope

Sections:

- Project Challenge: articulate the business/design problem.
- Design Strategy: explain layout, light, materials, storage, circulation.
- Materials Used: stone, veneer, brass, linen, oak, microcement, glass.
- Moodboard: 6-tile grid of materials and atmosphere references.
- Gallery: 8-12 images with consistent crop rhythm.
- Before/After: comparison slider.
- Testimonial: single large quote.
- Outcome/Results: 3 stats such as storage improved, natural light, delivery timeline.
- Next Project navigation.

Sample project copy:

### Modern Villa

Challenge: `The villa had generous volume but lacked warmth, flow, and a consistent material language.`

Strategy: `We introduced a grounded palette of walnut, stone, linen, and brushed brass, then used layered lighting to soften the scale of each room.`

Outcome: `A calm, gallery-like home that feels expansive without feeling empty.`

### Smart Penthouse

Challenge: `The client wanted advanced automation without visible technology disrupting the interior.`

Strategy: `We concealed smart controls, integrated ambient lighting scenes, and used reflective surfaces to amplify skyline views.`

Outcome: `A high-functioning penthouse that feels effortless, cinematic, and deeply personal.`
