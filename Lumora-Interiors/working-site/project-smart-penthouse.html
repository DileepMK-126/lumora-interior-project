Smart Penthouse

Invisible automation, skyline drama, and a refined palette built around effortless daily control.

Location

Mumbai

Scope

Smart Home

Year

2026

Category

Renovation

Challenge

Advanced technology without visual noise.

The client wanted automation, lighting scenes, and integrated audio without disrupting the calm penthouse atmosphere.

Strategy

Conceal the system, reveal the experience.

A high-functioning penthouse that feels effortless, cinematic, and deeply personal.
