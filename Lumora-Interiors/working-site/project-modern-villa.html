Project Showcase

Modern Villa

A warm, gallery-like villa shaped through walnut, stone, linen, brushed brass, and layered light.

Location

Bengaluru

Scope

Full Home

Year

2026

Category

Residential

Challenge

Generous volume without warmth.

The villa had impressive scale but lacked flow, intimacy, and a consistent material language. The brief asked for a home that felt expansive without feeling empty.

Strategy

A calm material rhythm.

We introduced walnut, stone, linen, and brushed brass, then used layered lighting to soften transitions between lounge, dining, and private rooms.

Before / After

From disconnected rooms to a composed sequence.

The final home feels calm, social, and quietly luxurious.

Outcome

34% more usable storage, stronger natural light, and a home that finally feels resolved.

“Every detail felt deeply considered.” - Anika Rao
