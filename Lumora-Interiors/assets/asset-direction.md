# Asset Direction

## Image Style

Use interiors that feel architectural and editorial:

- Warm natural light.
- Stone, walnut, brass, linen, glass, oak, travertine.
- Strong vertical and horizontal architectural lines.
- Human presence is optional and should be subtle.
- Avoid over-staged, cluttered, or colorful stock imagery.

## Suggested Image Set

Homepage:

- Hero: cinematic luxury living room or villa lounge.
- Featured Projects: villa, penthouse, Scandinavian apartment.
- Before/After: renovation transformation pair.
- Gallery: mix of living room, kitchen, bedroom, workspace, material details.

Pages:

- About: studio workspace or designer reviewing moodboard.
- Services: room-specific imagery.
- Portfolio: 5 project cover images.
- Contact: refined material or studio desk image.

## Alt Text Pattern

Use descriptive, natural alt text:

- `Luxury modern living room with walnut wall panels and warm ambient lighting`
- `Minimal Scandinavian apartment dining area with oak furniture and soft neutral palette`
- `Smart penthouse lounge with city view and concealed lighting`

## Asset Naming

Use lowercase kebab-case:

- `lumora-hero-living-room.webp`
- `modern-villa-cover.webp`
- `smart-penthouse-before.webp`
- `smart-penthouse-after.webp`
